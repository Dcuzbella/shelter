FEATURES

Our features and services

Safe Shelter

We provide a secure and confidential living space where women and their children can find safety from abuse, violence, or homelessness.

Counseling and Emotional Support

Our experienced counselors offer individual and group therapy sessions to help women heal from trauma, build resilience, and develop healthy coping mechanisms.

Life Skills Training

We offer workshops and classes on various life skills, including financial literacy, job readiness, parenting skills, and self-care, to empower women to lead independent and fulfilling lives.
